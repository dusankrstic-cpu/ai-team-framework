## How to Request a Retrieval

If you need information that may have been archived:

1. Note your request in your role's output document:
   - **PD:** in the strategic log or a directive
   - **DD:** in DECISIONS.md
   - **Team:** in your report under "Open Questions"
2. Ask the dispatcher to start a Documentation Optimizer session with your search query
3. Results will be written to `ARCHIVE/RETRIEVAL_RESPONSE.md`
